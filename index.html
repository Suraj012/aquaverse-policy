Introduction

Welcome to AquaVerse. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information to provide you with a cinematic and personalized experience. This includes:

  • Account Information: Data used for synchronization across your devices via Firebase.
  • Biological Database: Images captured or uploaded via your camera for pathogen identification.
  • Technical Data: Device identifiers and usage patterns for performance optimization.

Third-Party Services

To provide advanced features, AquaVerse utilizes several trusted third-party providers:

Google Firebase Backend infrastructure, authentication, and database synchronization.
Google Gemini AI Processing of biological data and descriptive analysis via generative AI.
RevenueCat Secure management of in-app purchases and subscriptions.
Google AdMob Personalized advertisement delivery based on device identifiers.

Device Permissions

AquaVerse requires the following permissions to function as intended:

  • Camera: To capture images of biological specimen for the database.
  • Storage: To save or upload images from your device gallery.
  • Internet: To synchronize data with the abyssal cloud and access AI capabilities.

Data Security

We implement the highest industry standards for data encryption and security. Your biological database and personal records are synchronized using end-to-end secure connections provided by our infrastructure partners.
